About Joey

I am a student in the Myra Kraft Transitional Year Program at Brandeis University and I am pretty musical. I play the Ocarina, Guitar, and a little bit of Piano. All of these instruments are self-taught. I am also a gamer which is why I am making this app. I enjoy shooting games, action games, fighting games, horror games (such as Resident Evil) and more. The idea is not completely original. There was a website called Mugen where you could do the same thing but I want to make this into an app with better graphics and a wider variety of characters.
